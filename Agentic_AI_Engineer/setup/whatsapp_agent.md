I will be writting a short report on my progress with setting up the ai agent on Whatsapp. Overall, I successfully integrated the OpenClaw AI agent to automatically reply to my WhatsAPP messages whenever entering into OpenClaw gateway. This was achieved once I have resolved the network connectivity issues between the WhatsApp gateway and OpenClaw. Ultimately, I switched the underlying LLM from a local Ollama (llama 3.2) setup to OpenAI's GPT-5.5 because OpenClaw and Ollama were experiencing integration issues.  

Once I received the agent's responses, I recognized how its replies were never received by WhatsApp the times I continuously opened Openclaw gateway in the terminal and sent myself a WhatsApp message. I was able to witness the full loop-around of how my message was received and then how the agent responded and received on my end. 

Another observation I would like to note is how personable the model is. It is more dependent and able to come up with replies to get me to answer back rather than me taking full control in conducting the conversation. I think this model would be an effective real-estate AI agent with further work and understanding of how the model works.
